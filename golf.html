Marilyn Rea/Foundation Golf Tournament

Every year we have an annual golf tournament, named in memory of Marilyn Rea who passed away several years ago. she was on the Santa Clara Unified School District Board of Trustees and was very active at her children's schools.

Tournament Information

Place
Santa Clara Golf and Tennis Club
Format
4 Person Scramble
  • handicap division
  • non handicap division

Invitations are open to both women and men golfers. The tournament is limited to the first 144 golfers. Sign up your own foursome or singles will be matched to form a foursome.

Included With Entry Fee

  • Golf & Cart
  • Tee prizes
  • Refreshments on the course
  • Chicken/Ribs BBQ buffet at David's Restaurant
  • Tournament prizes
  • Door prizes

Special Tournament Awards

  • Closest to the Hole
  • Longest Drive
  • Hole-in-One

How to Enter

Please download the registration form and mail it in with a check to cover the amount to the following location:

Santa Clara Schools Foundation
1889 Lawrence Rd.
Santa Clara, CA 95051

Where Do We Get Our Funding?

The Foundation funding comes from three major sources:

  • The Annual Campaign
  • The Marilyn Rea/Foundation Golf Tournament
  • private donations from local corporations, small businesses, community friends and Santa Clara Unified alumni.

How is the Money Spent?

All money raised from the Marilyn Rea/Foundation Golf Tournament and private donations goes towards our grants and scholarship programs.

All money raised from the Annual Campaign will go to fund programs and services that have a direct impact on students, such as maintaining class size reduction, music programs, technology, and counseling. 10% of the money will go to the Foundation's endowment fund to support our district's long-term educational goals.

Annual Campaign

The annual campaign has become a fundraising fixture in many public school foundations across the nation. Many of our neighboring cities have successfully raised hundreds of dollars per student through their annual campaigns allowing them to largely offset budget cuts. Our schools are in a crisis and we know that our community wants to help. During the annual campaign, we will seek funds from district families, all residents of Santa Clara, and local businesses.

Marilyn Rea/Foundation Golf Tournament

Every year we have an annual golf tournament, named in memory of Marilyn Rea who passed away several years ago. she was on the Santa Clara Unified School District Board of Trustees and was very active at her children's schools.

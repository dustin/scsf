What is the Annual Campaign?

The annual campaign has become a fundraising fixture in many public school foundations across the nation.

Many of our neighboring cities have successfully raised hundreds of dollars per student through their annual campaigns allowing them to largely offset budget cuts. Our schools are in a crisis and we know that our community wants to help. During the annual campaign, we will seek funds from district families, all residents of Santa Clara, and local businesses.

How Will the Money Be Spent?

All of the money raised will go to fund programs and services that have a direct impact on students, such as maintaining class size reduction, music programs, technology, and counseling. 10% of the money will go to the Foundation's endowment fund to support our district's long-term educational goals.

What Can I Do to Help?

The Foundation is asking all district families to make a tax-deductible donation.

Please use this form for details on how to contribute. The suggested amount is $100 per student, but any amount you can give will make a difference.

If the company you work for has a matching gift program, you can double your contribution. Please contact your HR department and take advantage of this program.

If you are interested in volunteering your time to help the Foundation, please email Laura Richardson or call her at (408) 735-1689.

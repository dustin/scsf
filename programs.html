Grant Program

Our goal is to provide assistance to programs that encourage quality academic performance, give students hands on experiences and promote their interest in learning.

Grants are made available through a selective applications process. After a grant proposal has been received the decision regarding the projects of merit and the award of grants is made by the Santa Clara Schools foundation Board of Directors.

Grant Policy

The Santa Clara Schools Foundation makes grants to classroom teachers for learning materials, equipment, programs and activities that directly benefit our students.

The Foundation does not make grants for personnel related functions, programs that involve on-going funding or foreign travel. Proposals for grants should consist of a short letter identifying the requesters, the number of students who will benefit from the proposal, the time frame, the amount requested, the total cost and the extend to which other fund raising activities or sources of income are available to fund the project. You can either send in the letter or apply online.

One of our objectives is to help children who are unable to afford the cost of field trips, although funding for field trips will be limited. For example, if your class needs $1000 for a field trip, we ask that you raise a portion of that amount through fundraisers and ask the Foundation to fund the balance. Not only does this help the foundation to use its funds effectively, but it engenders a sense of accomplishment and pride on the part of your students

Our Board of Director meets monthly during the school year, so please submit your request at least sixty days before the planned activity expenditure. To be considered for discussion and possible approval, the grant request must be received one month prior to our monthly meeting.

Request for curriculum material should supplement and not supplant the district's core curriculum. New materials shall be in accordance with district curriculum standards.
